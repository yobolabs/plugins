# Trap Library — Known Campaign Failure Patterns

Patterns the devil's advocate treats as guaranteed considerations when they match a mission's shape. Two kinds of entries:

- **SEED rules** — platform mechanics and classic failure classes, included from day one (marked `[seed]`).
- **EARNED entries** — from YOUR real campaigns, added via simulations (`simulate` skill) and after-action reviews. These are the valuable ones. Format:

```
### <id> <one-line name>
- What you see: <the early signal>
- Why it happens: <the real cause>
- What to do: <the move that works>
- Source: <campaign name/date — real incidents only>
```

Class files: `deliverability.md` · `whatsapp-platform.md` · `segmentation.md` · `creative-brand.md` · `offers-pricing.md` · `timing-frequency.md` · `data-hygiene.md` · `measurement.md`

Rule: an earned entry without a real campaign behind it gets deleted. The library's authority comes from "this actually happened to us."
