# Traps — Measurement

### MS-01 [seed] Untrackable campaign
- What you see: "did it work?" answered with vibes
- Why: no UTMs, no unique code, no way to attribute sales to the send
- What to do: measurement mechanics are a MOVE in every war-game (links tagged, codes unique per campaign/channel)
### MS-02 [seed] No holdout, no proof
- What you see: revenue went up — but it was payday week; nobody can say what the campaign did
- Why: everyone got the message, so there's nothing to compare against
- What to do: on deep-care campaigns, hold out a small slice; compare after
### MS-03 [seed] Watching the wrong number mid-flight
- What you see: team celebrates opens while unsubs quietly spike
- Why: watch-points defaulted to vanity metrics
- What to do: watch-points always include a HARM number (unsubs, complaints, rating) alongside the success number
### MS-04 [seed] Results never recorded
- What you see: next quarter, nobody remembers what this campaign actually did; the same mistake repeats
- Why: after-action section skipped once the campaign "felt fine"
- What to do: after-action is mandatory in the war-game file; the reviewer refuses exemplar status without it
