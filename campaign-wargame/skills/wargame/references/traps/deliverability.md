# Traps — Deliverability & List Health

### DL-01 [seed] The list burn
- What you see: unsubscribe/complaint spike on a big send; next sends reach fewer people
- Why: too much volume to too-cold contacts; complaints permanently damage sender reputation
- What to do: segment by warmth; cold contacts get a re-permission touch, not a promo blast; unsub watch-point with a pause threshold on every deep-care send
### DL-02 [seed] Spam-trigger wording
- What you see: low delivery/inbox rate despite clean list
- Why: ALL-CAPS, "FREE!!!", link shorteners, image-only emails
- What to do: creative check against spam patterns before send; plain-text alternative where supported
### DL-03 [seed] One bad send poisons the next three
- What you see: campaign after a flop underperforms with no obvious cause
- Why: reputation is a rolling score — platforms remember
- What to do: ripple map ALWAYS walks the list-health chain; after any complaint spike, deliberately send high-engagement content before the next promo
