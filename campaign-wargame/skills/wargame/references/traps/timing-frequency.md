# Traps — Timing & Frequency

### TF-01 [seed] Frequency fatigue
- What you see: declining opens across recent sends; rising unsubs
- Why: audience touched too often across ALL campaigns (not just yours)
- What to do: researcher pulls this audience's total contact count last 30 days across every channel; fatigue threshold respected
### TF-02 [seed] Timezone / prayer-time / mealtime miss
- What you see: dead send window, or complaints about badly-timed messages
- Why: send scheduled in the operator's timezone or during locally-sensitive hours
- What to do: send window stated in the AUDIENCE's local time with local calendar checked (holidays, Ramadan hours, weekends)
### TF-03 [seed] Calendar collision
- What you see: two campaigns land on the same audience the same day
- Why: no shared campaign calendar check
- What to do: researcher checks the calendar as a standard fact-base line
### TF-04 [seed] Drip step fires after the customer already converted
- What you see: "buy now!" message to someone who bought yesterday; feels robotic
- Why: flow has no exit rule on conversion
- What to do: every drip step lists its exit rules; the devil's advocate walks "what if they buy mid-flow?"
