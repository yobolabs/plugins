# Traps — Offers & Pricing

### OF-01 [seed] Discount stacking
- What you see: margin far below plan
- Why: new promo combines with an existing code/loyalty benefit nobody cross-checked
- What to do: researcher lists every LIVE offer this audience can already access; stacking rules stated in the fact base
### OF-02 [seed] The un-honorable promise
- What you see: redemptions exceed stock or budget cap; merchant must break the promise
- Why: no redemption cap in the offer mechanics
- What to do: every offer states its cap and what happens at the cap BEFORE send; redemption pace is a watch-point
### OF-03 [seed] Discount training
- What you see: full-price sales sag after repeated promos; customers wait for codes
- Why: promo cadence taught the audience the price is negotiable
- What to do: ripple map walks the discount-training chain on EVERY promo; scout proposes non-discount hooks where possible
### OF-04 [seed] "Last chance" that wasn't
- What you see: trust damage; complaints when the same offer returns two weeks later
- Why: urgency language used as decoration
- What to do: urgency claims are can't-take-it-back promises — only used when literally true
