# Traps — Creative & Brand

### CB-01 [seed] The unbackable claim
- What you see: merchant upset post-send; edit requests after delivery (impossible)
- Why: creative says "best", "guaranteed", "#1", or a health/financial claim the brand can't defend
- What to do: claims check against brand rules as a pre-flight on every can't-take-it-back send
### CB-02 [seed] Stale price or sold-out hero product
- What you see: customers click and find a different price, or nothing in stock
- Why: creative built days before send; price/stock moved
- What to do: price + stock re-verified in pre-flight, not at design time
### CB-03 [seed] Broken or wrong link
- What you see: clicks but no landing-page traffic; or traffic to the wrong page
- Why: typo, wrong campaign parameter, landing page unpublished
- What to do: click every link in the test send, on a phone, logged out
### CB-04 [seed] AI-generated creative drift
- What you see: image or caption subtly off-brand (wrong dish, wrong vibe, uncanny details)
- Why: generated assets approved in bulk without a close look
- What to do: batch approvals sample-check at full size; anything with hands, text-in-image, or the merchant's actual product gets individual review
