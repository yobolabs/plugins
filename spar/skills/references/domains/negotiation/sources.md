# Negotiation — Sources

## Getting to Yes (Fisher & Ury, 1981; updated 2011)
Canonical frames: BATNA, interests vs positions, objective criteria. Dated on tactics; the frames endure.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Penguin, 3rd ed. 2011 · grade: A

## The Art and Science of Negotiation (Raiffa, 1982)
The analytical backbone: reservation prices, ZOPA, concession analysis, decision-theoretic prep.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Harvard UP, 1982 · grade: A

## Negotiation Genius (Malhotra & Bazerman, 2007)
Best single practitioner-grade synthesis of the HBS research program: process negotiation, MESOs, investigative negotiation, deal fever.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Bantam, 2007 · grade: B

## The Mind and Heart of the Negotiator (Thompson)
Standard research textbook; reliable meta-analytic summaries (fixed-pie, lose-lose rates) with primary citations.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Pearson, 7th ed. 2020 · grade: A

## Program on Negotiation (PON), Harvard Law School
Free research digests, case studies, and current tactic teardowns; good for refreshing stale entries.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: pon.harvard.edu · grade: B

## Never Split the Difference (Voss & Raz, 2016)
FBI hostage-negotiation practice: calibrated questions, tactical empathy, labeling. Practitioner anecdote, not controlled research — mine for questions and personas, not for effect sizes.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: HarperBusiness, 2016 · grade: B

## Friend & Foe (Galinsky & Schweitzer, 2015)
Cooperation/competition research in trade form by the first-offer researchers themselves; anchoring, perspective-taking, power.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Crown Business, 2015 · grade: B

## Primary journals: JESP, OBHDP, JAP, Psychological Science, Negotiation Journal
Where the effect sizes live (Orr & Guthrie 2005; Mason 2013; Moore 2004; Tuncel 2016; Curhan 2006/2007). Cite these over any trade book when a number is load-bearing.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: journal archives via Google Scholar · grade: A

## Kellogg Dispute Resolution Research Center (DRRC) materials
Source of the MESO literature and standard teaching exercises used to benchmark negotiation performance.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: kellogg.northwestern.edu/research/drrc · grade: B
