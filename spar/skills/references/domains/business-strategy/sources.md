# Business Strategy — Sources

## 7 Powers (Helmer, 2016)
The moat taxonomy + counter-positioning; the sharpest tool for predicting incumbent response.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Hamilton Helmer, 7 Powers: The Foundations of Business Strategy, 2016 · grade: B

## Good Strategy Bad Strategy (Rumelt, 2011)
The kernel (diagnosis/policy/action) and the bad-strategy diagnostics (fluff, goals-as-strategy). Best filter for whether a "strategy" is one.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Crown Business, 2011 · grade: B

## Porter's HBR corpus
"What Is Strategy?" (1996), "The Five Competitive Forces That Shape Strategy" (2008 update of 1979). Industry structure and trade-offs; still the baseline vocabulary.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: hbr.org archive · grade: A

## The Innovator's Dilemma / Innovator's Solution (Christensen, 1997/2003)
Disruption as mechanism; resource-process-values on why incumbents rationally lose. Apply with definition discipline (most "disruption" isn't).
date: 2026-07-06 · source: HBS Press · grade: A

## Zero to One (Thiel, 2014)
Distribution thesis, monopoly vs competition framing, contrarian-question discipline. Opinionated practitioner source — mine for attack angles.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Crown Business, 2014 · grade: B

## Golder & Tellis + JPIM literature
Peer-reviewed reality on pioneer advantage (1993) and new-product failure rates (Castellion & Markham 2013). The antidote to first-mover and 90%-fail folklore.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Journal of Marketing Research; Journal of Product Innovation Management · grade: A

## BLS Business Employment Dynamics
Official establishment survival tables by cohort and industry — the ground-truth survival base rates.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: bls.gov/bdm/bdmage.htm · grade: A

## CB Insights research + post-mortem series
Startup failure causes, market maps, funding data. Vendor research — directionally useful, methodology opaque; pair with BLS/academic numbers.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: cbinsights.com/research · grade: B

## Annual M&A scorecards: Bain M&A Report, BCG M&A Report, KPMG deal studies
Rolling empirical checks on deal outcomes; refresh the 70–90% destruction prior with current-year numbers (Bain 2025: ~30% met targets; BCG 2026: 60% trailed market).
date: 2026-07-06 · source: bain.com, bcg.com, kpmg.com annual M&A publications · grade: B

## Innosight Corporate Longevity Forecast
S&P 500 tenure compression series (2016, 2018, 2021 editions) — the incumbency-decay base rate.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: innosight.com/insight/creative-destruction · grade: B

## Stratechery (Ben Thompson)
Aggregation theory, platform vs product economics, running teardowns of live strategic moves. Sharp practitioner analysis; opinions, not data.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: stratechery.com · grade: B

## Primary financial record: 10-Ks, S-1s, earnings transcripts
For incumbent-response ground truth and case forensics (Zynga 2012 10-K, JC Penney FY2012, Webvan S-1). Always preferred over press retellings when a case is load-bearing.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: SEC EDGAR (sec.gov/edgar) · grade: A
