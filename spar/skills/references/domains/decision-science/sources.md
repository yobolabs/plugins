# Decision Science — Sources

Where good information lives. Recon starts hunts here.

## Judgment Under Uncertainty: Heuristics and Biases (Kahneman, Slovic, Tversky, 1982)
Primary literature for anchoring, availability, base-rate neglect, calibration (Alpert & Raiffa). Cite the original papers, not popularizations.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Cambridge University Press, 1982 · grade: A

## Thinking, Fast and Slow (Kahneman, 2011)
Synthesis + inside/outside view. Caution: several priming/ego-depletion chapters failed replication — trust the judgment-and-choice chapters, verify anything social-priming.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: FSG, 2011; replication caveats per Kahneman's own 2017 blog concession · grade: A (with noted caveats)

## Expert Political Judgment (Tetlock, 2005) + Superforecasting (Tetlock & Gardner, 2015)
The forecasting-accuracy evidence base; scoring rules, calibration training, team structures that work.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Princeton UP 2005; Crown 2015 · grade: A

## Flyvbjerg's project-outcome datasets
Largest empirical base on plan-vs-actual (cost/schedule/benefits) — JAPA 2002, HBR 2011 (IT), How Big Things Get Done (2023, 16,000+ projects). The go-to for overrun base rates.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: flyvbjerg.oxfordsaidalumni.org; arxiv.org/abs/1304.0265 · grade: A

## Noise (Kahneman, Sibony & Sunstein, 2021)
Judgment variability across judges/occasions; noise-audit method; decision hygiene protocols (independent-then-aggregate).
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Little, Brown 2021 · grade: B

## Thinking in Bets + Quit (Annie Duke, 2018/2022)
Practitioner translation: resulting, kill criteria, quitting discipline. Use for framing; cite academic originals for numbers.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Portfolio 2018, 2022 · grade: B

## Judgment in Managerial Decision Making (Bazerman & Moore)
Standard textbook; reliable summaries of escalation, overconfidence, bounded awareness with citations to primaries.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Wiley, 8th ed. 2012 · grade: A

## Good Judgment Open / Metaculus
Live crowd-forecast base rates on current events; use to price "what's the probability of X" claims during a spar.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: gjopen.com; metaculus.com · grade: B

## McKinsey Quarterly behavioral-strategy stream
Lovallo & Sibony corpus on decision process in the wild. Consultancy self-report — use for direction, not precision.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: mckinsey.com "The case for behavioral strategy" 2010 · grade: B

## Replication-crisis filter (standing caution)
Before using any social-psych effect as a load-bearing attack, check its replication status (e.g., Many Labs projects, replicationindex.com). Anchoring, base-rate neglect, overconfidence, sunk cost: replicated. Priming-class effects: assume fragile.
date: 2026-07-06 · source: Open Science Collaboration, Science 2015 (36% of 100 psych studies replicated) · grade: A
