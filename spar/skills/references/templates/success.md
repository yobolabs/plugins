# Acceptance Criteria (seed — copy per mission folder, tighten per mission)

## Mission mode — a war-game is accepted only when ALL hold

1. **Runs cold** — the HUMAN executor could run it with zero additional context: every action literal (what to say/send/sign), every observation something a person can see, hear, or count; no "as discussed"; no unstated background.
2. **Every move complete** — action + success observation + failure observation + most-likely reaction (cause + signals, from the actor whose payoff drops most) + both countermove branches. Success and failure observations discriminable, or the discriminating probe (with deadline) given.
3. **Reality pressure real** — failures plausible, grounded (fact, pack entry, or base-rate citation), and discriminable from each other. A war-game where no move fails is rejected outright.
4. **Depth walked = depth claimed** — order numbers on every consequence finding; chains shown one step at a time; landing times noted; honest terminations (`map-exhausted` distinct from "no consequences"). Depth knob reached or the shortfall explained. Zero depth theater.
5. **Epistemic integrity** — no untagged load-bearing claim; `verified` means cited + dated from this run; A/B/C grades on web claims; unknowns closed only by hunts; survivors in the ledger with failed hunts listed, never dropped.
6. **Both directions walked** — opportunity register present and Better-ranked; every "later" has a promotion trigger, every "designed-for" a named seam; risk-only drafts rejected.
7. **First-principles pass done** — inherited constraints challenged once; any "it's the way it is" converted to either a verified reason or an opportunity entry.
8. **Fork triggers & aborts** — divergence observables defined before execution; abort conditions specific, observable, human-checkable, present at the end.
9. **Taste** — the war-gamed route itself scores on the Better rubric; if a simpler route to the objective exists, the draft either takes it or shows why not.
10. **Active-instrument comparison** — any detector/tripwire move prices at least one ACTIVE evidence-manufacturing alternative (direct ask, paid pilot, outreach) and beats it on evidence-per-day and evidence-quality — or takes it. A detector whose expected time-to-verdict exceeds the mission's own time constraint fails Taste (item 9).
11. **Payment-grade evidence** — a mission whose objective is revenue contains at least one move that requests payment; a free instrument measures politeness/curiosity, not demand.

## Spar mode — a spar is well-run only when ALL hold

S1. **Steelman confirmed** by the user before the first attack, logged in the state file.
S2. **Attack quality** — ≥ 90% of attacks clear the floor: attributed + evidence-classed + falsifiable. Struck attacks counted.
S3. **Closed verdicts** — survives/wounded/dead only, one per attack surface, every verdict carrying its flip condition. Zero open-vocabulary verdicts.
S4. **Concession symmetry** — every defeated attack explicitly declared dead; conceded ground never re-litigated; a transcript where red-team never loses a point is flagged as theater.
S5. **Degraded discipline** — zero `dead` verdicts on `unresearched` evidence; all caps listed at close.
S6. **Honest ending** — the spar ends in a position delta or an evidence-backed "survives" verdict — never polite agreement.
S7. **State complete** — all five state-file sections present and current; the spar is resumable; the judge drift audit ran at close and its violations were reported to the user.

## Calibration gate (framework run scored against a Fable-direct run)

| Dimension | Gate |
|---|---|
| Failure-branch coverage | ≥ 80% of the direct run's distinct attack surfaces / failure branches |
| Consequence depth | achieved order ≥ direct's on the same moves; zero depth-theater findings |
| Opportunity findings | ≥ 2/3 of the direct run's register, or gap items recorded in capability-gap.md |
| Epistemic hygiene | 0 untagged load-bearing claims; 0 unknowns closed without a hunt trail |
| Attack quality | ≥ 90% of attacks pass the quality floor |
| Verdict integrity | 0 rubric violations |

Two consecutive held-out missions passing all gates = ship.
